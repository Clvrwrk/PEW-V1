About Us with sub-pages: Mission, Pro Ministries Outreach, Operations & Leadership.

Blog/Resources Hub — category filtering, search, featured posts.

Blog Post (template) — long-form SEO content with reverse silo internal linking.

Contact Us — dual-path form (commercial RFQ vs. residential inspection), phone, map.

Customer Portal — Centerpoint SSO integration, utility nav link.

Careers — open positions, culture, benefits.

**Navigation:** Home, Services (mega menu: Commercial Hub, Residential Hub, ProPlan), Locations, Projects, About (dropdown), Resources, Contact. Utility nav: Portal Login, Phone.
