# Accessibility Statement

Pro Exteriors is committed to providing a website accessible to the widest possible audience, including individuals with disabilities. This site targets WCAG 2.2 Level AA compliance.

## Standards we follow

- Color contrast ratios meeting WCAG AA across body text and interactive elements
- Keyboard navigability for every interactive control
- Visible focus styles on all focusable elements
- Skip-to-main-content link on every page
- Tap targets at least 44 × 44 px on mobile

## Reporting an issue

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this site, contact us at (469) 535-1708 and we will work to resolve it.
