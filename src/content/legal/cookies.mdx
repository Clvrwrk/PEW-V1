# Cookies Policy

Pro Exteriors uses cookies to operate this website, measure traffic, and improve the experience.

## Categories of cookies we use

- **Essential cookies** — required for the site to function (form submission, accessibility preferences).
- **Analytics cookies** — Google Analytics 4 and PostHog. We use these to understand which content is useful and to improve the site.
- **Functional cookies** — remember preferences such as your nearest office.

## Managing cookies

You can disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings. Doing so may affect site features.
