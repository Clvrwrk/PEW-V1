## What Commercial Roof Replacement Actually Involves

Complete roof replacement begins with structural assessment and existing system removal. Our crews evaluate deck conditions, identify necessary repairs, and document any code compliance issues. Removal includes old membrane, insulation, and damaged decking materials. We coordinate debris removal to maintain site cleanliness and safety protocols.

Insulation installation follows manufacturer specifications for R-value requirements and vapor barrier placement. We install polyiso, EPS, or XPS insulation based on climate zone requirements and building performance goals. Proper insulation attachment prevents wind uplift and thermal bridging that compromises energy efficiency.

Membrane installation varies by system type but follows identical quality standards. TPO welding requires certified technicians and specialized equipment to achieve watertight seams. EPDM installation demands precise adhesive application and proper lap joint preparation. Modified bitumen systems need controlled torch application and careful overlap management.

Flashing and penetration details determine long-term performance. We fabricate custom flashings for HVAC units, vents, and roof drains using compatible materials. Pipe boots, equipment curbs, and parapet caps receive individual attention to prevent water infiltration. Each penetration gets tested before final inspection.

Final inspection includes comprehensive leak testing and warranty documentation. We conduct flood testing on critical areas and provide detailed warranty certificates from both Pro Exteriors and material manufacturers. Project closeout includes maintenance recommendations and emergency contact procedures.
