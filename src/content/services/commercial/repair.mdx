## What Commercial Roof Repair Actually Involves

Commercial roof repair starts with comprehensive damage assessment using thermal imaging and moisture meters. We identify not just visible damage but hidden moisture intrusion that leads to structural problems. Our technicians document everything with photos and measurements for insurance claims and facility management records.

Repair scope varies dramatically by roofing system. Single-ply membrane repairs involve heat welding or chemical bonding of patches using Carlisle, Versico, or Duro-Last materials matching your existing roof. Modified bitumen repairs require torch application or cold adhesives. Built-up roofing repairs involve removing damaged layers and rebuilding the system with hot asphalt and reinforcing fabrics.

Metal roof repairs address fastener failure, panel damage, and flashing issues. We source matching panels and use McElroy Metal components for seamless integration. Coating applications with Soprema or Manta systems provide additional protection and extend roof life by 10-15 years.

Project sequencing minimizes operational disruption. We schedule work during off-hours when possible and coordinate with facility managers to protect sensitive areas. Weather monitoring ensures repairs happen during optimal conditions. Emergency temporary protection keeps your building dry while permanent repairs are planned and executed.

Material procurement happens through established partnerships with GAF, CertainTeed, and Atlas. We maintain inventory of common repair materials and can source specialty components within 24-48 hours. This supply chain reliability means faster repairs and consistent quality across all projects.
