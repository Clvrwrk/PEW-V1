## What to Expect with Commercial Roof Repair

The specifics of a commercial roof repair vary by roof membrane material, damage cause and assembly age. We service every category of commercial roof repair on TPO, EPDM and all of our other single-ply roof systems, including modifications: built-up roofing, modified bitumen, flat roof coatings (like SPF) and exposed fasteners metal roofing.

We can perform a typical repair on most TPO single-ply membranes in the same way we repair a TPO single-ply membrane, by cleaning off the top layer, slicing the area to access and dry it, then applying or welding new material into place with a patch. We only use materials sourced from Duro-Last, Versico and Carlisle to ensure it has the same composition as the original and bonds to form an unbroken seal. We can patch our modified and built-up roof installations with Soprema and Atlas products where they're part of the existing roofing materials.

Repairing flashes for any roof penetrates-such as on pipes, HVAC units, parapet walls and rooftop drains-are one of the most common roof repair tasks and a major point of entry for many leaks when the job isn't completed professionally. Improperly flashing any penetration on your commercial roof is one of the most common causes of interior damage claims across North America. After every roof repair our field managers close out, each repair detail is thoroughly checked by the supervisor.

On metal roof repairs, it requires a bit of a different skillset. If there are problems on your metal roof system that don't involve simply popping off and replacing one panel, we can align a clipped panel back into place or completely remove the old sealant, then reapplied with proper methods and sealants. Depending on how far along in deterioration the roof is, sometimes the bolts holding the panels need replacing or tightened and we can work on such systems if they were manufactured by either Sentrigard or McElroy Metal. We maintain an inventory of metal panels to avoid a multi-week lead time on more standard roof repairs on those specific systems.

Finally, before any repair work can begin, one of our inspectors can provide a visual or infrared survey of the property's roof membrane to calculate the full extent of the roof's problem. After that, our inspectors will document any flaws they see on your roof system by providing photographs, taking measurements of anything concerning and submitting the findings to you in a written report, this report will look similar to the one you'd receive from an insurance adjuster.
