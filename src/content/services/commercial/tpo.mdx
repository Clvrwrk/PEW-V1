## What TPO Roofing Installation Actually Involves

TPO membrane installation requires precise coordination between substrate preparation, insulation placement, and membrane attachment to achieve the thermal and waterproofing performance your building envelope demands. Our process begins with complete roof deck evaluation, including core sampling to verify structural capacity and moisture content levels that could compromise membrane adhesion or thermal performance over time.

Substrate preparation involves power washing, crack sealing, and primer application to create the clean, stable surface essential for long-term membrane performance. We install polyiso or EPS insulation boards using manufacturer-specified fastening patterns, ensuring consistent R-values across the entire roof area while maintaining proper drainage slopes toward designated drain locations.

Membrane installation follows strict manufacturer protocols for seam welding, using hot-air welding equipment calibrated daily to maintain the precise temperatures required for molecular bonding between membrane sheets. Our certified welders complete destructive seam testing throughout the installation process, ensuring every linear foot of seam exceeds minimum peel and shear strength requirements before project completion.

Flashing details around penetrations, edges, and equipment require custom fabrication using compatible TPO materials and proven attachment methods. We coordinate closely with mechanical contractors to ensure proper equipment curbing and flashing integration, preventing the penetration failures that account for most commercial roofing warranty claims during the first five years of service.

## Our TPO Installation Process

1. **Pre-installation Assessment**: Complete roof deck evaluation including moisture surveys, structural analysis, and existing system documentation review to identify potential complications before mobilization begins.

2. **Substrate Preparation**: Power washing, crack sealing, and primer application create the stable, clean surface required for optimal membrane adhesion and long-term performance.

3. **Insulation Installation**: Polyiso or EPS boards installed using manufacturer-specified fastening patterns, maintaining proper R-values and drainage slopes throughout the roof area.

4. **Membrane Installation**: Hot-air welded TPO sheets installed according to manufacturer specifications, with continuous seam testing and quality verification at each installation phase.

5. **Flashing and Detail Work**: Custom fabricated TPO flashings around all penetrations, edges, and equipment, coordinated with mechanical trades for proper integration.

6. **Final Inspection and Warranty Activation**: Comprehensive system testing including flood testing of critical areas, followed by manufacturer warranty registration and project documentation delivery.
