### The Scope of Emergency Roofing

Emergency roofing isn't just a simple fix, it is a sequence of steps beginning with stopping water damage and concluding with a repair or replacement that meets stringent warranty requirements. Here's how this unfolds on a real project:

1. **Water Mitigation: ** If your roof is actively leaking, the immediate priority is installing a heavy - duty polyethylene tarp. This tarp is secured with battens strips, avoiding staples or heavy sandbags which can cause their own damage. A properly installed tarp can withstand additional weather events, granting us sufficient time to plan the complete repair without being pressured by impending storms.

2. **Damage Assessment: ** After we've secured the affected area, we conduct a complete assessment of the roof system. This includes examining the decking, underlayment, flashing, ridge, valleys and penetrations. Hail damage is almost never limited to the visible shingles from the ground, we use high - definition drone footage and close, detailed inspection methods to thoroughly document every facet of the damage. We also look for granular loss patterns that can indicate the age and intensity of storm impact.

3. **Material Selection: ** For repairs to asphalt shingle roofs, our primary materials are GAF Timberline HDZ and CertainTeed Landmark Pro shingles, selected to match the existing roof profile as closely as possible. We favor synthetic underlayment over traditional felt paper because it tolerates moisture much better during the period while we schedule and perform the full repair. We also use Atlas and Soprema materials for repairs on any low - slope sections on residential roofs, such as patio awnings or flat - roof extensions.

4. **Repair Execution: ** All repairs are performed precisely to the manufacturer's specifications - not merely to the minimum acceptable standard to satisfy the insurance company. We meticulously inspect and replace, as needed, the ridge cap shingles, step flashing around walls and chimneys and pipe boot seals - beyond just the damaged shingles themselves.

5. **Final Inspection and Documentation Close - Out: ** We conduct a comprehensive review of the completed repair with you, providing a detailed written scope of work. We also deliver the photo package your insurance provider requires to process and close out your claim.
