### What does a residential roof replacement entail?

A complete residential roof replacement goes far beyond a simple re - cover job. The process initiates with a complete tear - off of all existing roofing materials down to the underlying roof deck. This is the crucial step that allows us to thoroughly inspect the deck's condition, address any rot, delamination or structural weaknesses and make necessary repairs before installing new materials. Cutting this corner to save time on labor is exactly how contractors create warranty - voiding layered installs that trap moisture and subsequently lead to premature roof failure.

After the roof deck is clear and any damaged sheathing is replaced at the established cost, the installation follows a well - defined sequence. First, ice - and - water shield is installed along the roof's eaves and valleys. Next, synthetic underlayment is applied over the main field of the roof, followed by the installation of your chosen primary roofing material. In most residential projects, this primary material consists of architectural asphalt shingles. We predominantly use GAF Timberline HDZ and CertainTeed Landmark shingles. Both of these top - selling products are rated for Class 4 impact resistance, an important consideration in Texas where hail storms are a frequent occurrence and in areas like Colorado. Another excellent alternative for homeowners primarily concerned about algae growth in humid environments, such as those found in Atlanta, is Atlas Pinnacle Pristine.

The most frequent failure point for residential roof replacements lies in the flashing. The proper installation of step flashing along walls, kickout flashing where the rake wall meets the wall and correctly sealed pipe boots is an absolute prerequisite on every Pro Exteriors project. We don't reuse existing flashing on a full roof replacement, as doing so is a cost - cutting measure that can create significant liability issues for the homeowner within a three to five - year period.

We meticulously evaluate and document every job for proper ridge ventilation, soffit intake ventilation and overall attic airflow. Subpar ventilation not only nullifies manufacturer warranties but can also shorten the lifespan of your shingles by several years. We carefully record ventilation measurements and include them in your project file for your records.
