## Serving DFW from Arlington

Located right in the heart of the metro, Arlington serves as our hub for an expanse that covers many directions.

From this branch, crews reach south and southeast across Grand Prairie, Mansfield, Kennedale and Pantego.

Traveling north and northeast, teams cover Fort Worth's Near Southside and Cultural District neighborhoods, then head over to Irving, Las Colinas and Coppell.

To the east of Tollway 121, Arlington provides roofing services in Euless, Bedford, Hurst and Colleyville.

For large - scale commercial work, teams work closely with Pro Exteriors headquarters in Richardson to provide services throughout Plano, Garland, Mesquite and all of the eastern DFW region.

Should your business or home be located within either Tarrant County or Dallas County, it's the team working out of Arlington that will be managing your project.

## Commercial & Residential Under One Roof

There are many tangible operational benefits to keeping the commercial and residential verticals at the same office.

In Commercial Roofing, our experienced project managers manage projects ranging from HOAs, strip centers and restaurants to major retail sites and multi - site industrial campuses.

We provide single - point - of - contact service across the entire scope of commercial re - roofing and repair.

Pro Exteriors' Commercial Roofing Division has obtained manufacturer certifications required to offer NDL warranties on TPO, EPDM and modified bitumen roof systems.

On the Residential side, the estimator walking your home's roof is directly involved throughout the entire project - they're the individuals creating the scope and managing the install.

Pro Exteriors' Residential Roofing specialists offer architectural asphalt shingles, synthetic slate roofing and standing - seam metal roof systems expertly installed for DFW's high winds and hail.

Underneath both residential and commercial divisions, you'll find the same warranty team, material procurement partnerships and stringent quality control inspections.

## Materials and Services

The Arlington team installs and repairs low - slope and steep - slope roofing systems:

* **TPO and EPDM membrane systems: ** For flat roofs and extensive commercial buildings, including new installations and repairs to existing seams
* **Asphalt architectural shingles: ** Offering Class 4 impact - rated shingles appropriate for the severity of DFW hailstorms
* **Metal roofing: ** Including standing - seam and exposed - fastener systems for both residences and small commercial operations
* **Modified bitumen: ** Two - ply torch - down systems or cold - applied multi - ply modifications for low - slope roofs
* **Storm damage assessments and insurance claims documentation: ** staffed by licensed insurance adjusters than third - party individuals
* **Preventive maintenance plans: ** Our clients receive annual roof inspections accompanied by written status reports

Across every type of service, Pro Exteriors' Arlington maintains consistent estimating and project management workflows, no scope or phase is handed off mid - process.
