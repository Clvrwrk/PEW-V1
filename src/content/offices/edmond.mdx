Edmond covers northern OKC's metro area and surroundings, including Edmond proper, Deer Creek, Guthrie, Yukon, Mustang, Piedmont, Bethany and extends crews into Midwest City, Del City and Moore, along with Choctaw and Harrah. Whether you are in a newer development around Coffee Creek and 33rd St or one of our long - standing commercial account customers with businesses near downtown Edmond, you'll receive the same continuity in our work crews.

## Two doors, one office - commercial + residential

Both commercial roofing and residential roofing are operated from this office without them being placed into a separate silo. We manage commercial roofing work for low - slope and flat systems that cover such facilities as retail strips, medical offices and warehouses. Project managers dispatch crews and handle all coordination directly with property owners, facilities directors and general contractors, bypassing unnecessary regional contacts. Residential roofing crews handle shingle replacement or upgrade to high - definition designer shingles for homeowners looking to protect their investment from a storm or an aging roof. All roof maintenance programs are also handled here. Both types of roofing business operate with consistent estimating procedures and consistent warranty paperwork to provide a streamlined experience to property owners that manage both commercial and personal property under one point of contact.

## What we install and repair from here

From this location we install and repair the following systems:
* Three - tab and architectural shingles, along with Class 4 impact - rated asphalt shingles specifically developed to withstand Oklahoma hail.
* TPO and EPDM membrane roofing for all low - slope and flat commercial roof projects.
* Standing seam metal and exposed - fastener roof panels for residential structures as well as agricultural and commercial buildings.
* Detailed repair reports, photo documentation packages and adjuster communications for customers experiencing storm damage to their roof.
* Maintenance plans including scheduled inspection check - ups, flashing reseals and drainage clears to prevent potential roof damage.
* Corrected roofing vents or decking issues as part of a complete replacement where an old deck or ventilation system has either begun to fail per code or is no longer in compliance with manufacturer specifications.

Materials are chosen based on Oklahoma's extreme climate, not chosen from a general catalog of the best - selling roofing materials across the country.
