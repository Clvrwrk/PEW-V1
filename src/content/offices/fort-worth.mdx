### What we install and repair from here

Pro Exteriors Fort Worth dispatches roofers for the following 6 service categories:

* **Asphalt shingle installation and replacement: ** Includes the installation of high - impact, Class 4 shingles to meet the insurance requirements in Texas for wind and hail.
* **TPO and EPDM flat roofing: ** Covering a new flat roof installation, seam repair of a flat commercial roof or replacement by removing a flat commercial roof down to the deck.
* **Metal roofing: ** With both standing - seam and exposed - fastener options, we can provide Metal roof installation for your home or business.
* **Storm damage repair and insurance documentation: ** Following a hailstorm or high wind event, Pro Exteriors will come inspect your roof and create an itemized repair proposal with detailed photos designed to satisfy any adjuster from an insurance carrier.
* **Roof maintenance programs: ** Through scheduled biennial inspections and repairs for items like flashing and seals on the roof, our roof maintenance program will extend the life of your flat commercial roof or asphalt shingle roof for years, protecting against future leaks.
* **Gutters and exterior drainage: ** seamless aluminum and steel gutters are professionally manufactured, designed for each property and integrated into your overall roof system by a roof team at Pro Exteriors or as a standalone service.
