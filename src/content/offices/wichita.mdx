Pro Exteriors Wichita supports roofing work across south-central Kansas, where hail, wind uplift, and fast-moving storms shape the roof specification. The team handles commercial assessments, residential inspections, emergency stabilization, repair, and replacement.

For commercial properties, Wichita is focused on membrane systems, metal roofing, repair, and replacement. For homeowners, the office prioritizes asphalt shingles, Class 4 impact-resistant upgrades, storm documentation, and emergency leak triage.

Start with a commercial RFQ or residential inspection request and include the roof age, known damage, and timing constraints.
