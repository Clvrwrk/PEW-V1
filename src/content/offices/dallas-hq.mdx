Pro Exteriors Dallas based in Richardson, TX, the Pro Exteriors Dallas office functions as the primary nerve center for our roofing operations, serving not only Dallas - Fort Worth but also four additional major metropolitan markets. This centralized hub is where all estimates are developed, all projects are managed and all warranty support is coordinated for both commercial and residential roofing customers. When you pick up the phone or come to this address, you're speaking with the team directly involved in the entire project lifecycle from start to finish - not a call center sending you on a wild goose chase.

From this Richardson HQ, Pro Exteriors covers the entire Dallas - Fort Worth metroplex, encompassing Uptown and Downtown Dallas, Plano, Frisco, McKinney, Allen, Garland, Mesquite, Irving, Grand Prairie, Arlington, Fort Worth and the Mid - Cities corridor, including Euless, Bedford and Hurst.

Furthermore, we dispatch our specialized field crews and project managers from this location to each of our four satellite markets in Denver, CO, Wichita, KS, Kansas City, MO and Atlanta, GA.

While each satellite market possesses dedicated on - the - ground leadership, all estimating, procurement and warranty administration activities are channeled through Dallas.

This setup ensures that pricing remains consistent and accountability for each job remains firmly with our central team, irrespective of the job's geographic location.

The Dallas office is equipped to handle both the commercial and residential facets of roofing under a single roof, a significant advantage for customers with mixed property portfolios.

Property managers who oversee diverse assets such as retail strip malls, HOA common areas and portfolios of single - family rental homes don't need to maintain relationships with multiple roofing contractors.

Our commercial roofing division specializes in a wide range of services, including low - slope membrane systems like TPO, EPDM and Modified Bitumen, along with metal standing - seam systems for various commercial properties including office parks, warehouses and multi - tenant retail establishments across DFW.

Conversely, our residential roofing division focuses on high - quality architectural shingles, impact - resistant upgrades to mitigate hail damage and potential insurance premium discounts in hail - prone zip codes throughout DFW and comprehensive storm - damage replacement services for homeowners residing in communities from Prosper down to Mansfield.

Both the commercial and residential teams adhere to identical estimating standards, procure materials from the same established suppliers and follow a consistent warranty documentation process, leading to cost savings throughout the lifecycle of each roofing project, whether it's for 40 units or just a single - family dwelling, promising predictability, documented work and no unpleasant surprises upon renewal.

Crews working out of Dallas are proficient in and regularly deploy the following six core service lines:

* **Asphalt shingle replacement and repair: ** This includes the installation and servicing of Class 4 impact - resistant shingles, which may be eligible for reduced insurance premiums in hail - heavy areas of Dallas - Fort Worth zip codes.
* **TPO and EPDM membrane systems: ** We install and repair these essential systems for flat and low - slope commercial roofs throughout the Dallas - Fort Worth metroplex.
* **Metal roofing: ** We provide expertise in both standing - seam and exposed - fastener metal panels for a variety of commercial and residential roofing needs.
* **Storm damage assessment and insurance coordination: ** We develop and provide meticulously documented inspection reports that help smoother and faster processing of insurance claims.
* **Roof coatings and restoration: ** We offer fluid - applied coatings that extend the service life of existing membrane roofs, often eliminating the need for a costly full tear - off and replacement.
* **Gutters and drainage systems: ** We install these crucial components in conjunction with roofing scopes to effectively protect the fascia, soffits and foundation of structures.

To maintain transparency and professionalism, every project we undertake begins with a detailed, written inspection report. Verbal estimates aren't provided and there will never be any undisclosed additions tacked onto your bill at the final closing.
