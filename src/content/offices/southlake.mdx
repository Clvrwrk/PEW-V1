Pro Exteriors Southlake our North Texas hub is situated at Pro Exteriors Southlake, located in the mid - cities corridor, which permits our teams access to all Dallas - Fort Worth suburbs and major cities. Commercial and residential crews licensed and insured throughout Texas operate from this hub and coordination for multi - market projects happens through this office in conjunction with our Denver, Wichita, Kansas City and Atlanta locations.

### Service Area From This Office

Southlake rests at the absolute center of a high - concentration service corridor. We routinely deploy teams to neighborhoods throughout northern Texas (Flower Mound, Argyle, Denton), eastward (Grapevine, Colleyville, Irving), southward (Arlington, Mansfield, Grand Prairie) and westward into the Fort Worth areas of the Near Southside, Cultural District and the Alliance Corridor. Our services extend as far into Dallas proper as Uptown, Preston Hollow, Lake Highlands and the Design District. Suburban neighborhoods such as Keller, Hurst, Euless, Bedford and Coppell lie squarely within our typical dispatch radius. We partner with already embedded field supervisors for major commercial jobs in Frisco, McKinney or Plano, but if your commercial or residential property lies within the Dallas - Fort Worth metroplex, this office is responsible for your roofing project. ### Two Doors, Same Office - Commercial and Residential Services

We run both divisions under one roof. This setup means that when a property manager who's in charge of a Grapevine shopping center and a Keller homeowner dealing with hail damage contact Pro Exteriors, both customers connect with a knowledgeable team specifically trained for their projects. For the commercial segment, this office handles TPO, EPDM and metal panel roofing on various retail, industrial and multi - family projects. Our project managers are adept at deciphering and processing insurance documentation, phased replacement project scopes and the lifecycle cost analysis that facilities directors and owners require when looking at capital expenditures. For residential customers, project consultants meticulously guide them through the insurance claim process, discuss different materials, set realistic installation timelines and maintain the level of specific attention they demand. Discover the complete spectrum of [commercial roofing services](/commercial-roofing/) and review the offerings from our [residential roofing division](/residential/) before your first call.

### Installation & Repair Services Offered From Southlake

The following six service areas are managed from our Southlake office by specialized, extensively trained teams:

* **Asphalt Shingle Roofing: ** Full replacements and targeted repairs for homes and small commercial buildings.
* **TPO and EPDM Membrane Roofing: ** These heat - welded, single - ply membranes are used on flat and low - sloped commercial roofs and come with full manufacturer warranties.
* **Metal Roofing: ** We install standing seam, concealed fastener systems and exposed fastener panels for both residential and commercial clients.
* **Storm Damage Assessment and Repair: ** We accurately document wind, hail and impact damage to adhere to carrier requirements in one of the nation's most active weather zones.
* **Roof Coatings and Restoration: ** We install silicone and acrylic systems on commercial roofs to restore their condition and extend the membrane's life before a full replacement is necessary.
* **Gutters and Drainage Systems: ** seamless aluminum, galvanized steel and copper gutters are installed as a component of any complete exterior scope.

Every installation from the Southlake office is covered by the manufacturers' material warranties and the Pro Exteriors workmanship guarantee, both fully documented at the project's conclusion.
