## Competitive Patterns Summary

### Positioning Approaches

**Systems-First (CentiMark):** Leads with material types and technical specifications; appeals to technical decision-makers

**Heritage & Values (Baker Roofing):** Emphasizes 110+ year history and employee ownership; warm, values-driven messaging

**Consultative/Integrated (Flynn Companies):** Positions as "building envelope" expert, not just roofing; offers design assist and proprietary tools

**Specialty Services (Greenwood):** Diversifies with historic restoration, campus care, drone inspections; appeals to niche markets

**Public Sector (Best Contracting):** Organized by sector; strong public sector/government credentials; leadership transparency

### Service Packaging

- **All sites** mention maintenance + repair + new work (the 3-pillar model)
- **Greenwood & Best** expand beyond roofing (waterproofing, metal, historic work)
- **Flynn** uses "building envelope" language (comprehensive vs. roofing-only)
- **Baker** explicitly targets both homeowners AND commercial (dual positioning)

### CTA Strategies

- **Quote requests** are the dominant CTA (Baker, Best)
- **Phone numbers** are always prominently featured (ranges from 1-800 to local 310 area codes)
- **Contact forms** are standard (CentiMark, Flynn, Greenwood)
- **Emergency hotlines** are differentiators (CentiMark 24/7 line)
- **Proprietary apps/tools** (Flynn F.A.S.T., Greenwood RoofWatch) serve as engagement/loyalty mechanisms

### Trust Signals Hierarchy

1. **Heritage/Longevity** (Baker: 1915; implied by size)
2. **Employee Ownership/Culture** (Baker distinguishes itself)
3. **Licenses/Bonding/Insurance** (All mentioned; more prominent in Best)
4. **Proprietary Programs** (Greenwood, Flynn use these)
5. **Safety Programs/Academy** (All mention; Baker emphasizes "safety first" culture)
6. **Project Portfolio** (Flynn, Best leverage visually)
7. **Awards/Media** (Best & Greenwood mention; less prominent elsewhere)

### Tone Consistency

All sites use **professional yet warm** language. None are transactional or aggressive. All emphasize:
- Long-term relationships ("Always do good work")
- Safety culture
- Reliability/heritage
- Customer care

### Target Audience Segmentation

- **CentiMark:** Facility managers, property managers (commercial-only focus)
- **Baker:** Homeowners + commercial (dual market approach)
- **Flynn:** Commercial GCs, architects, facility directors (consultative positioning)
- **Greenwood:** Educational institutions + commercial (campus care niche)
- **Best:** Government, education, healthcare sectors + commercial GCs (public sector specialist)
