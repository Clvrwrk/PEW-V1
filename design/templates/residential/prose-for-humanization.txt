Coverage depends on your policy type — RCV (replacement cost value)
pays the full replacement minus your deductible, ACV (actual cash
value) depreciates the payout based on roof age. Either way, document
damage immediately after a storm; most carriers enforce a one-year
filing window and some enforce 180 days. We handle insurance
documentation, adjuster meetings, and supplement filings as part of
the project — not as an upsell. Hail damage repair claims are the
single most common residential job we run across Texas, Kansas, and
Colorado.

Two warranties on every job. The manufacturer warranty (GAF
System-Plus or Golden Pledge, Owens Corning Platinum, depending on

materials. The Pro Exteriors craftsmanship warranty covers the
installation labor — lifetime on most products, transferable to the
next owner if you sell the house. Property-linked records make the

Three deciding factors: roof age, damage pattern, and shingle
granule loss. A roof under ten years old with isolated damage is
usually a repair candidate. A roof over twenty years old with
widespread granule loss, multiple leak points, or hail hits across
more than 30% of the field is typically a replacement. The free
inspection and photo report give you the answer in writing, not as
